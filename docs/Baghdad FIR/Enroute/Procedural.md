In the west of the Baghdad FIR, within the North sector an area, with limited radar coverage, is controlled procedurally not under a radar control service.. This area is defined as follows:

A radar control service is provided in all controlled airspace, and along all airways within the Baghdad FIR.
An exception to this is segments of airways G202, L200 and R652 which have limited radar coverage and is controlled procedurally. This is defined as follows:

> RAPLU to MODIK on AWY G202;
> GIBUX to PASIP on AWYL200;
> GIBUX to DAXAN on AWY R652.
