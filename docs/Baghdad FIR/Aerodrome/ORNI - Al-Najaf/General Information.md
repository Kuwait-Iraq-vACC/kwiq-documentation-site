# General Information
**Al-Najaf International Airport (ORNI/NJF)** is an international airport serving Najaf, Iraq. The airport is located on the eastern side of the city and was formerly a military airbase. It is an important international gateway for Najaf and supports significant religious, passenger, and airline traffic.
